# Moment of Inertia
Moment of Inertial is resistance to change of state of rotational motion

MOI depends on mass, distribution of mass w.r.t axis, axis of rotation
$$
I = \Sigma m_{i} r_{i}^{2}
$$ *Continuous body*
$$
I =  \int r^2 \, dm 
$$
