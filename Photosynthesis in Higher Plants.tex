\section{What is Photosynthesis ?}\label{what-is-photosynthesis}

Anabolic, enzyme regulated (biochemical) formation of organic materials
from CO2, H20 by green parts of the plant (chlorophyll containing) in
presence of sunlight, thereby liberating O2, leads to autotrophic
nutrition, removes CO2 and generates oxygen, 170 billion tonnes of
biomass/year. 70-80 million tons of CO2 is fixed by terrestrial plants.
